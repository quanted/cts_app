<b>EPI Suite<sup>TM</sup> (Estimation Program Interface)</b> - EPI Suite is a Windows-based suite of physical/chemical property and environmental fate estimation programs developed by EPA and SRC, formerly Syracuse Research Corporation.<br><br>

<!--<a href="http://www.archemcalc.com/sparc.html" target="_blank" class="fadeMenu">SPARC Performs Automated Reasoning in Chemistry</a> -- SPARC is a physicochemical calculator  that uses computational algorithms based on fundamental chemical structure theory to estimate a wide variety of reactivity parameters strictly from molecular structure. <br><br>-->

<b>ChemAxon</b> - ChemAxon provides cheminformatics software platforms, applications and services to optimize the value of chemistry information in life science and other research and development.<br><br>

<b>Toxicity Estimation Software Tool (TEST)</b> - TEST was developed by EPA to allow users to easily estimate the toxicity of chemicals using Quantitative Structure Activity Relationships (QSARs) methodologies. The software also contains QSAR models for a number of physicochemical properties.<br><br>

<b>OPERA</b> - OPEn structure-activity/property Relationship App (OPERA) is a QSAR tool for predicting physicochemical and environmental fate properties.<br><br>
